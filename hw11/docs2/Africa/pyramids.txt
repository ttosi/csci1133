The Giza Pyramid Complex, also called the Giza Necropolis, is the
site on the Giza Plateau in Greater Cairo, Egypt that includes
the Great Pyramid of Giza, the Pyramid of Khafre, and the Pyramid
of Menkaure, along with their associated pyramid complexes and
the Great Sphinx of Giza. 

All were built during the Fourth Dynasty of the Old Kingdom of 
Ancient Egypt.
